### Week 5 Objectives:
* Learn about Amazon RDS and supported database engines (MySQL, PostgreSQL, SQL Server).  
* Practice creating, connecting, and managing RDS databases from EC2 or Spring Boot applications.  
* Understand RDS features such as snapshot, backup, Multi-AZ, and Read Replica.
